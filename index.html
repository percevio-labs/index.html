Research & Innovation

Cutting-edge AI research powering real-world solutions

Our team has published at top-tier conferences (ICCV, WACV, AAAI) and built state-of-the-art multimodal AI systems. These breakthroughs inform the technology we bring to enterprise clients.

BioPose: Biomechanically-accurate 3D Pose Estimation

WACV 2025

Real-time, biomechanically accurate 3D human pose estimation from monocular video— enabling healthcare, sports analytics, and AR/VR applications without specialized hardware.

View Paper →

GenHMR: Generative Human Mesh Recovery

AAAI 2025

Probabilistic 3D mesh reconstruction that handles depth ambiguity and occlusions by modeling multiple plausible hypotheses—critical for robust video understanding at scale.

View Paper →

LiveGesture: Streamable Co-Speech Gesture Generation

Preprint

First fully streamable, zero-latency gesture generation framework for lifelike avatars and real-time human-AI interaction—no look-ahead, arbitrary length, region-coordinated motion.

Hierarchical autoregressive transformer with streamable motion tokenizer for causal, full-body gesture synthesis.

MaskHand: Generative Masked Modeling for Hand Reconstruction

ICCV 2025

Robust 3D hand mesh reconstruction in challenging, occluded scenarios using masked modeling— powering gesture control, AR interactions, and sign language recognition.

View Paper →
